A B O U T   M E

 

Hi, my name is Hoang. I was born in 1999 and raised in Vietnam. I'm a CG Generalist and specialized in Lighting & FX who pursues perfection through hard work.

I'm also a Flat Designer and interested in Motion Graphics. I have a huge passion for 3D and VFX so I studied CGI on my own to bring imagination to life.

Follow my dream and I learned many techniques by working on many different computer software. Graphics and learning new things have always been an addiction and a good way to express myself.

My goal is to never stop learning and to be able to share my abilities with teams who have particular skills in order to create amazing things together.

One thing for sure: If you come and talk to me about science fiction, computer technology, music and dreams, piano and guitar, animals and nature, we can be more than simple friends.
